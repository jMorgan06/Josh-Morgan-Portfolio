After Life

Date Completed: April 6th, 2023

My second year game during University I made as part of the Game Development Workshop course. A horror survival game called After Life. I was largely responsible for the environments, textures and level design.

Responsible For

  • Environment & Set Dress
  • Level Design
  • Textures

Game Description

'Once the day is done and your life is over, you can relax and drift off into dreamland. For most that's where your day ends, but for some it's only the beginning of a brand new nightmare. You have one goal, and that's to sleep through the night. Through the various horrors that your mind can concoct in your subconscious, you're your own greatest danger in Banana Hoshi's newest title After Life.'
