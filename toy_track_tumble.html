Toy Track Tumble

Date Completed: April 4th, 2025

A game I worked on in the second half of my fourth year in university as part of the Game Development Workshop course. I was responsible for the background environment/cube map and textures within it, as well as most enemy car models (Mustang GT, Miata, Formula 1).

Responsible For

  • Background Environment/Cube Map
  • Car Models
  • Textures
  • Lighting

Game Description

'Toy Track Tumble is a 3D Arcade Racing game. Using a variety of both cars with fantastic abilities, and special weapons, battle and race against other drivers in an ever evolving track. A game for people who love arcade racing games, this game will put a bit of a unique spin on the traditional racing game formula, as you battle against hordes of endless racers.'
