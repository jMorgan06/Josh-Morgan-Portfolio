About Me

Hello I'm Josh,

I really love 3D modelling and animation. I've dedicated the past 3 years to learning and researching 3D animation. Ever since I started using Blender I haven't been able to put it down. It has allowed me to harness my passions for making really cool things, and now I'm building a career out of it.

I am a vehicle artist,

Which means I specialize in modelling mostly cars and motorcycles, but I will do other types of vehicles as well. I do both exteriors and interiors. I especially love applying materials and lighting as that's when I can really start seeing the beauty of my work. I also find setting up the environments very pleasing as I love seeing what stories I can tell with just the surrounding objects.
